üö´ Anti-Cheating Policy & Fair Play

Zero Tolerance for Gaming the System

The Top Zombie Challenge is designed to reward plebs for cleaning up their legitimate follow lists. Any attempts to manipulate, inflate, or artificially game your zombie kill count will result in immediate disqualification.

Prohibited Activities Include:

  • Follow Inflation: Artificially inflating your follow count between purges by mass-following accounts solely to increase your kill count
  • Backup Manipulation: Editing or falsifying backup files
  • Multiple Entries: Creating multiple accounts to enter the competition
  • Bot Activity: Using automated tools to inflate follows or purges
  • Coordinated Manipulation: Coordinating with others to artificially boost scores

Verification & Investigation

We monitor all competition entries for suspicious patterns, including but not limited to:

  • Unusual spikes in follow count between purges
  • Inconsistent zombie percentages across multiple purges
  • Abnormally high kill counts relative to account history
  • Multiple large purges in short time periods

If you are suspected of cheating, you will be required to:

  1. Submit ALL backup files from your Backups tab for analysis:
    • Click the Backups tab in Plebs vs. Zombies
    • Press the Export button for each backup
    • Share the exported JSON files via pastebin.com or similar service
    • OR email the files to: brains@plebsvszombies.cc
  2. Provide verification of your Nostr account history and follow patterns
  3. Respond to investigation inquiries within 48 hours

Failure to cooperate with verification requests will result in automatic disqualification.

Disqualification

Users found to have violated fair play policies will be:

  • Immediately removed from the leaderboard
  • Disqualified from prize eligibility
  • Banned from future competitions
